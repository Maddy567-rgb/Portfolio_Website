MADHAV SORAL

I’m a video editor with a flair for innovative design and a passion for experimentation. I push creative boundaries to craft dynamic, captivating visuals. Always eager to explore new techniques, I elevate videos to new heights and make a lasting impact.

ABOUT ME

I’m Madhav Soral, a 21-year-old visual storyteller skilled in tools like Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, Photoshop, Canva, and After Effects. I began my journey in editing back in 12th grade, driven by a desire to create AMVs. Over the years, I discovered that editing was more than just a hobby—it was my true calling. This realization led me to join Aevy TV, where I refined my craft even further. With a sharp eye for detail and a passion for creativity, I specialize in crafting visual experiences that resonate deeply and elevate videos to another level, enhancing brand narratives and captivating audiences.
